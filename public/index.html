01 — Intake
~60 sec
AI Matter Package
Enter client name and situation. Walk out with a complete case summary memo, full engagement letter with e-sign, jurisdiction-specific deadline calendar, and a 15-item 30-day action checklist. What used to take 3 hours takes 60 seconds.
02 — Research
~2 min
Legal Researcher
Ask any legal question in plain language. Get a structured research memo with case citations, majority/minority rules, jurisdiction-specific analysis, and a clear strategic conclusion. The quality of a Westlaw search. The price of zero.
03 — Drafting
~90 sec
Motion & Pleading Drafter
Select from 12 document types — demand letters, motions to dismiss, discovery responses, trial briefs, and more. Choose your tone. Link to a matter. Get a complete, court-ready draft tailored to your specific case facts.
04 — Trial Prep
~3 min
Deposition Prep Kit
Enter the witness and your goal. Get 10 opening questions, 25 targeted core questions, 8 commitment/trap questions, documents to introduce, objections to anticipate, and a full strategy memorandum. What used to take half a day.
05 — Review
~2 min
Document Analyzer
Paste any contract, lease, settlement agreement, or pleading. Get a risk rating, flagged issues by severity, missing clauses, negotiation points, and a clear recommendation. Never sign off on a document you haven't fully analyzed.
06 — Valuation
~1 min
Settlement Calculator
Input practice area, liability strength, and special damages. Get a low/mid/high value range with factor-by-factor breakdown, comparable case analysis, and a negotiation strategy. Know your number before you pick up the phone.
07 — Compliance
Instant
SOL Lookup + Conflict Check
Look up every statute of limitations in any state with exact code citations, tolling rules, and calculated filing deadlines from your incident date. Screen every new client against your full matter history automatically. Never miss either again.
08 — Billing
Always on
Time Tracker + Billing Estimator
Log billable hours per matter with a start/stop timer. Generate phase-by-phase fee estimates for any matter type before engagement. Export to CSV for invoicing. Know what your time is worth and track every minute of it.
09 — Client
One click
Client Portal + E-Sign
Share matter documents with clients via a secure read-only link — no client login required. Collect e-signatures on engagement letters directly in the platform. Your clients get a professional, Big Law experience. You spend zero extra time.
10 — Intelligence
Per matter
AI Matter Assistant
Every matter has a dedicated AI chat with your full case file pre-loaded. Ask about deadlines, strategy, draft language, next steps. The AI already knows your client, the facts, the jurisdiction, and the opposing party. No re-explaining.
